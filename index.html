Quick calibration

Re-centers the sticks automatically in a few seconds. Don't touch the controller during the process.

Guided calibration

A 4-step procedure moving the sticks to the corners. More accurate on marked drift.

Range calibration

Recalibrates the maximum travel: rotate the sticks along the edge to cover the whole perimeter.

Precision test

A roughly one-minute mini-game: steadiness, targets and tracking. Score 0–100 to compare before and after calibration.

Calibration active but temporary. It's lost when the controller powers off: write it to memory to make it permanent.

Guided center calibration

    

      
    

    

      
      
This procedure re-centers the sticks by sampling their resting position after each movement.
        Once started it cannot be cancelled: don't close the page and don't disconnect the controller.

    

    

      
      
    

    

      
      
    

  







  

    
Range calibration

    
The controller is sampling. Slowly rotate both sticks while pressing them all the way
      against the edge, at least 2 turns one way and 2 the other. Keep working the areas where the trace
      stays short.

Write to the controller's memory?

    
The current calibration will be saved permanently in the DualSense's non-volatile memory (NVS)
      and will stay active on PS5, PC and everywhere.

    
Identical procedure to dualshock-tools', proven on millions of controllers. If in doubt
      you can always recalibrate again.
